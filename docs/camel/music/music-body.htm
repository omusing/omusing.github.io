Camel Sheet Music

A Camel Song Book was published by Chappel in 1979. The song book contains the following songs:
  • Breathless
  • Dunkirk
  • Echoes
  • Highways of the Sun
  • Lady Fantasy
  • La Princesse Perdue
  • Lunar Sea
  • Never Let Go
  • Rainbow's End
  • Rhayader
  • Rhayader Goes to Town
  • Skylines
  • Song Within a Song
  • Unevensong
  • White rider
