DAVID PATON
David was one of the founder members of Pilot along with Stuart Tosh. He was both lead singer and writer of their biggest hits, "January" and "Magic".
Since then David has been involved as a song writer and session player working on all the Alan Parsons Project albums, and most recently he has played on the music for the film "Death Wish II", with Jimmy Page. He also played on Kate Bush's first two albums.
