STUART TOSH
Andy's first major band was Arthud Brown's Kingdom Come where he met the other members of what was to become Vapour Trails. Vapour Trails became a recognised backing band for artists like Kiki Dee, Gus Dudgeon, Vic Smith and Elton John. They also recorded their own album for WEA, produced by Larry Carlton. More recently Andy has co-produced Jack Green and Brian Chatton for RCA Records.
