CHRIS RAINBOW
Although Chris is essentially a solo artist he has been involved in various projects with other artists.
After recording numerous jingles for Capital Radio and Radio 1 he recorded several albums for Polydor and EMI Records.
In between recording Chris toured with Jon Anderson in Europe and performed on 3 Alan Parsons albums. He has recently completed the music for an EMI video concept production.
