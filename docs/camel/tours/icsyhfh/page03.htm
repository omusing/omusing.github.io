ANDREW LATIMER (Guitar)

Whilst hesitant to reiterate yet another bumbling biography of his past, Andrew admits to being the inevitable protege of a musical family.
Pushed and cajoled into music lessons, he preferred to teach himself the theories and practical aspects of the guitar, forming his first band with his brother at fifteen.
Soon after Andrew went pro with Andy Ward in Brew a blues outfit that was to be the nucleus of CAMEL.
