Saxophones, clarinet and flute

Mel's credits range from sessions with obscure Icelandic groups the likes of George Harrison and Eric Clapton. He has toured with King Crimson, Alexis Corner, Alvin Lee, Kokomo and Bryan Ferry to name but a few. His close association with Camel began with "Moonmadness", and his exceptional talents are heard throughout "Rain Dances". Mel is fast becoming a fifth member.
