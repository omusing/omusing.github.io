Drums and Percussion

While Andrew Latimer's tastes are catholic - ranging from Sibelius to the Beach Boys - Andy Ward is the one who picks up on the sounds around at the time. As peter puts it, "He seems to embody all the trends of the last ten years!" He has been described as having "a thin, aesthetic appearance," but this is very much in contrast with his strong, punchy drumming.
