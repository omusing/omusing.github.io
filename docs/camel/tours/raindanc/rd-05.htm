Keyboards

As a one-time member of the legendary Irish R&B band, Them, Peter Bardens is assured his place in rock & roll history. Since those distant days, he has evolved his own distinctive sound on keyboards and synthesiser: witness his work on Mini Moog and String Synthesis on "Rain Dances". Melody Maker readers voted him among the ten best keyboard players in last year's poll. With Andrew Latimer, Peter co-writes all of Camel's material.
