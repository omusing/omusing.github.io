    This is the second CP release of live visuals, originally surfaced for the Curriculum Vitae documentary. As in the first Footage DVD, the bulk of the videos comes from the band's early incarnations, displaying the strength of the band on stage, and presents for the first time an official live recording of Air Born and Highways of the Sun. The latter tracks show the band in the rare moments of the 'acoustic set', from the 2000 tour, as well as a charming rendition of Fox Hill from the 2003 Farewell Tour.
