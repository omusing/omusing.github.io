Camel's third Official Bootleg was recorded on 13th June 1982, once again in Holland, and was chosen as an exemplary 10th anniversary tour for being a particularly delightful performance for the band. The CD sleeve details who sang solo in what songs and who played the guitar solos.
This CD has been recently been deleted by Camel Productions. You can order it before it runs out of stock.

Originally recorded by a Dutch radio station, the master had been destroyed, and this CD was mastered from a tape recorded by the sound engineer Pete Ward. Latimer consider this source "less then desirable" from a production standpoint, as "the bass end is not quite up to scratch because it was recorded directly through the mixing desk."
Three other songs which were performed at that night, Remote Romance, Rhayader Goes to Town and No Easy Answer were beyond salvage and do not appear on the CD.
