This Video captures a whole evening of Camel's Harbour of Tears tour, as performed on March 13th, 1997 at The Billboard Live club in Hollywood.
VHS tapes available in PAL or NTSC in Dolby Surround Sound.
DVDs available in NTSC in Dolby Surround Sound.

The video sleeve was designed by Chris Cavalier and Marc Lisle, Camel fans who also intoruced their own design for Camel's 1998 Tour Poster (check it out at their web site)
The DVD edition of Coming of Age contains more than 24 minutes of bonas footage shot during rehersals and sound check. The footage captures Latimer and the band as they work out the live interpretation of the studio songs.

Total Playing Time: VHS - 2 hours, DVD - 2:20
