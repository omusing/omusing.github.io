An Anthology of Camel from PolyGram/Deram Records, being the fifth and probably not the last Camel collection. Double CD set includes material from Camel to Dust and Dreams. All the songs are from their original releases, none of them from the live versions.

Susan Hoover wrote the liner notes.
Deram 844 340-2, PolyGram 44340

Bootleg Alert: An illegitimate bootleg double CD of poor sound quality, carrying the same title, is also out there. Make sure you're buying the DERAM/POLYGRAM CD, by identifying the DERAM label or by checking the sound quality of the recorded material BEFORE you purchase it.
