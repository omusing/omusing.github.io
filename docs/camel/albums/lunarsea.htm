This double-CD is another attempt to make the ultimate Camel collection, this time by compiling the band's songs under the concept of "The Decca Years", thus leaving out the output from the 90's. The elaborate liner notes are reportedly less compromising than those of the CP-endorsed Echoes collection. This collection stands out for featuring a rare live recording of Lady Fantasy.

The live recording of Lady Fantasy is a previously unreleased track, originally performed in the 1975 orchestral of the "Snow Goose" at the Royal Albert Hall, This orchestral version was released without CP's approval or control.
