Curriculum Vitae
2003

Curriculum Vitae presents the story of Camel from its early days of its predecessor Brew, through the climactic success of The Snow Goose, the lineups changes that followed Doug Ferguson's departure, and the revival in the 90's. The program was originally created for British Channel 4 TV series "Top Ten", to which Andrew Latimer, Andy Ward and Peter Bardens were interviewed. The program was extended by Camel Productions, by incorporating more interviews and video material. The highlights of the DVD are some rare live video footage from the various incarnations of the band.

The DVD contains an extra 12 minutes interview with Latimer, Ward and Ferguson, who got together in 2003 and worked out a new non-Camel album, to be released later in 2003 or early 2004.

Total Playing Time: Main Program - 68 minutes, Bonus Material - 12 minutes
