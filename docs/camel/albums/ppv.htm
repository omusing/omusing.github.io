A video of the show from the Pressure Points album, which took place at London's Hammersmith Odeon in 1984. The live video recording is combined with short, directed "movie" clips, supposedly bounding the whole songs of the performance to the concept of the Stationary Traveller album, about refugees and Travellers. Some may find these movie flicks a bit irritating. In my copy there were some disturbing sound dropouts, but the sound quality is generally better then the Total Pressure video.

Throughout the show, Stationary Traveller album is performed almost in its entirety.

Currently available on DVD from Camel Productions. Originally released on LaserDisc and VHS by PolyGram and VHS by Camel Productions.
Total Playing Time: 80 mins
