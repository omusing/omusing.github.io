A 25th Anniversary Compilation for Camel from Deram Records. This, being the 6th Camel compilation finally features a few "rare" tracks. The 18 songs spans Camel's releases in Deram from Mirage in 1973 till Pressure Points in 1984.
Highways of the Sun is the same bonus tracks as in the Rain Dances CD release. The Rainbow's End version is very simillar to the original. The live Never Let Go is apparently from A Live Record. The Another Night and Lunar Sea are the probably from the single mentioned in the Rarities section.
The album's title is simply "Camel", with the original group members' faces on the cover.

The CD includes liner notes by John Tracy.
