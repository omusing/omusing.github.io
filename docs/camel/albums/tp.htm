CP's first video bootleg... The Pressure Points video, without the added movie flicks in it, showing more of the band. Here the performance is presented unedited, from the start till the last encore, with a time code included (which isn't as annoying as it may sound!) One track is missing Long Goodbyes, although it is available on the Pressure Points video tape. On the other hand, this version features seven more songs, mostly from the early period of Camel. Beware - the sound and image quality are not as good as in the Pressure Points tape, but still very enjoyable. Peter Bardens appears on the last three tracks, but his solos annoyingly remained uncaptured by the cameras.

Originally released by Camel Productions on VHS. Currently not available.
Total Playing Time: 130 mins.
