    Following the unearthing of early video footage, as seen on Curriculum Vitae, Camel Productions were persuaded by fans to release this compilation of live video footage. Camel Footage presents, for the first time officially, footage from the band's early line ups of 1973 through 1977. Material from later years and various line-ups is also featured here, with the notable bootleg recording of exciting moments from the 1992 Dust and Dreams tour.
    The bonus track is an appetiser for future Reunion album by Camel's founder members Latimer, Doug Ferguson and Andy Ward.

    Total Playing Time: Main Program - 72 minutes, Bonus Material - 7 minutes
