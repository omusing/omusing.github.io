Camel Discography

This section lists all of Camel's releases - studio albums, official live recordings, compilations, singles and videos.

Each release is given some description (which is, to some extent, based on my personal taste), and some relevant information - participating musicians, trivia notes (denoted by , or by if they are a bit farfetched), and technical notes - such as Records, CDs and Cassettes serial numbers, album lengths etc.

References to relevant articles are also available, marked by .
