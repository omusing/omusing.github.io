Camel's second live record is a very good performance of the late 70's and early 80's songs, emphasizing the Stationary Traveller songs. The live versions are very good, some surpassing the original releases.
The album's back cover has pictures from the show.

Note Bardens guest-playing on two songs from The Snow Goose.
The songs were taken from a full-length live performance at the London Hammersmith Odeon, on the 11th May, 1984.
